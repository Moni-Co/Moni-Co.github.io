KICKSTARTING TOKEN ECONOMIES

Monico is the easiest and most complete platform to set up and participate in Initial Coin Offerings. Use your mobile device to create, invest, and trade tokens generated from ICOs around the world.

1. DISCOVER PROJECTS

Use the Project Directory to view all tokens and ICOs in the Monico ecosystem. Find new opportunities to support at a glance.

2. REVIEW THE PROJECT

Make informed decisions about participation in an ICO via the information and documents available in each company's Project Description. Some companies may also have audit reports from Monico partners, ensuring stringent assessment of the company's profile.

3. BUY TOKENS

Once you've decided to participate in a project, use your own crypto wallet or other payment partners to purchase tokens from a company or another person. For additional security, Monico never stores your private keys, and all transactions are decentralized.

4. WALLET CREATED

Wallets are created on your device for tokens that you purchase. Import and export wallets on the app, and trade securely through the arbitrated peer-to-peer trading system.

PAIN POINTS AND CHALLENGES

Some ICOs are

SCAMS

Nowadays, it is difficult to distinguish between ICO projects that are legitimate from those that are fake. Considering the large number of token sales emerging, there is no single venue for ICO participants to fully verify the legitimacy of an entity.

Web Exchanges are

CENTRALIZED

Exchanges are compromised on a regular basis due to the need for a central wallet to maintain liquidity between coins and tokens. Investors have to wait for tokens to be listed on exchanges before being able to participate in the secondary market, whereas companies have to pay large fees to a handful of exchanges in order to get their token listed.

ICO Participation is

COMPLEX

for beginners

The process of purchasing cryptocurrency, scanning for relevant projects, purchasing tokens, managing portfolios, and trading on secondary markets relies on many different pieces of software. For people wanting to participate in the new cryptocurrency economy, navigating this fragmentation of solutions can be a daunting task.
